## Assessment Scope

This repository focuses on the security assessment of selected web resources and email artifacts for educational and research purposes. The assessment includes:

1. **URL Analysis**
   - Identification of potentially malicious domains.
   - Threat intelligence cross-referencing with VirusTotal, PhishTank, and other security databases.
   - Flagging of URLs that exhibit phishing or malware behavior.

2. **Email Header Analysis**
   - Examination of sender and recipient information.
   - Verification of authentication protocols such as DKIM, SPF, and DMARC.
   - Identification of spoofing attempts or malicious origins.
   - Analysis of originating IP addresses and server paths.

3. **DNS and Network Checks**
   - MX, A, and PTR record inspections.
   - Evaluation of domain reputation and historical activity.

4. **Limitations**
   - Findings are based on publicly available tools and automated scanners.
   - This repository does **not** include internal network access or private data.
   - The analysis is **educational** and should not be used as the sole source for enforcement or legal action.

**Objective:** Provide clear, reproducible research for cybersecurity education, threat awareness, and phishing/malware analysis.
