## Mitigation Actions

### 1. Email Authentication Enforcement
- Implement and enforce **SPF, DKIM, and DMARC** policies for all sending domains.  
- Regularly monitor authentication reports to detect spoofing attempts.  

### 2. Endpoint & Network Controls
- Block or quarantine messages failing SPF/DKIM/DMARC at the mail gateway.  
- Enable advanced email threat protection, including **URL and attachment scanning**.  

### 3. User Awareness & Training
- Conduct ongoing **phishing simulations** and awareness campaigns.  
- Educate users on identifying **suspicious emails, links, and attachments**.  

### 4. Incident Response Integration
- Ensure email incidents are logged, triaged, and escalated per **IR procedures**.  
- Maintain an updated repository of **artifacts** for threat hunting and forensic review.  

### 5. Continuous Improvement
- Periodically review email security configurations and threat intelligence feeds.  
- Update internal policies and procedures to address **emerging email threats**.
